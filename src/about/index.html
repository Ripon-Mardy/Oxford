About us

Oxford Language Centre is an education and Visa advisors agency that specializes in providing superior support to people who want to travel abroad or study at one of the world’s most prestigious universities.

About Oxford Language Centre

Degrees in Various Academic Disciplines

In our commitment to keeping our students well-informed, we have taken the initiative to establish one of the few authorized computer-based Person BUE test centers in Morocco. Our primary objective is to offer an exam setting of professional standards and assist students in obtaining certifications. To achieve this, we have opened our centers in Ajman and Marrakech.

Our Vision

 The Oxford Language Centre is a renowned institution that provides language education, along with educational and immigration services, to students interested in studying abroad or traveling to one of the world’s most prestigious universities. Over the years, the center has built a strong reputation for offering exceptional services to clients who require guidance in navigating the intricate process of studying abroad.
