English Language Courses

General English

Our main goal is to provide students with well- organized and structured curriculum that help them improve their English studies and enlarge their vocabulary every day. We choose Headway book as a curriculum because we know that students will benefit the courses. The programme will give students a benefit strategy to enrich their oral and written vocabulary. Our institution provide highly qualified tutors who will support students by working on their listening, writing and speaking skills, in addition to the use of different materials and strategies. The teacher will assess and test his students with what they have done in every lesson.

Course Content

The course will focus on reading, writing and speaking skills of the student. Thus, each unit gives the students a great opportunity to improve his English language. Teachers will introduce students to each unit by using different methods and strategies that can facilitate the way of teaching and learning as well.

All these steps are associated with grammar rules that can help students express their thoughts, which are grammatically correct. Each unit gives a simple structure to help students use the easiest form of sentences and utterances. For beginner level, students will be familiar with the verb to be, to have, to go in simple tenses and situations. Moreover, when students succeed in each level, they will deal with advanced grammar; using complex sentences, perfect tenses and transform active sentences to passive sentences. Giving new vocabularies is very important in our courses. Teacher provide students a huge vocabulary related to what they will study in each unit in order to enhance students’ reading ,writing and speaking skills. Learning new vocabularies never complete without using technical methods to ease the work.

Study Method

Teacher will use different methods that facilitate work. These methods are formed as CD, videos, audios, flashcards, posters and other activities. Teacher try to link them in order to ease his work. As well as, students will learn easily and quickly.
